# Worked Example — Trading / DeFi Launch

**Project:** Velocity  
**Archetype:** Market Narrative × Protocol Breakthrough

## Core story angle

Retail traders do not trust execution because most apps talk about leverage and speed, not whether the fill was actually good.

## Example post

“Low fees” is not the same as good execution.

Most traders only discover that after the fact:

- the fill is worse than expected
- the slippage explanation is vague
- the interface still acts like everything is fine

Velocity is built around a simpler promise:

show the fill honestly and route small orders toward better outcomes.
